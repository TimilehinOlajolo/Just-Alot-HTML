Eggs are one of my favourite foods. HERE IS THE RECIPE FOR MY DELICOUSLY RICH SCRAMBLED EGGS
  • Ingredient
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 Tbs Butter
    • 2 Tbs Cream
  • Method
    • Heat the butter to melt it up in the frying pan
    • Gently Mix the eggs and cream in the bowl
    • Add the mixture to melted butter
    • Cook Egg, Fry.
